About

My name is Kelly, and I am a strategic designer dedicated to contributing to a sustainable and innovative future through strategic design. I believe that whether a design is product- or service-oriented, it should empower people to move towards a better future. Though the future is dynamic and ever-changing, I am convinced that through teamwork and careful analysis of companies, competitors, contexts, and consumers, a holistic vision can be developed to find the path to achieving the desired outcomes.

My design journey began at Delft University of Technology in the Netherlands, where I earned a Bachelor's degree in Industrial Design Engineering and a Master's in Strategic Product Design. The Master's program emphasises a contextual approach to market and user integration, focusing on creating products and services that are not only innovative but also feasible, usable, and desirable.

Studying at a technical university offered me a unique perspective, blending rigorous technical knowledge with creative design thinking. This combination has shaped my approach to design, where functionality and aesthetics meet real-world application.

During my Master's, I expanded my global perspective by studying abroad for a year. I spent a semester in Milan at Politecnico di Milano, where I delved deeper into design and even published a paper. I also studied Chinese at Fudan University in Shanghai, broadening my cultural understanding and perspective on the world.
