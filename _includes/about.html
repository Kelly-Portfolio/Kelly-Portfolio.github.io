About

My name is Kelly, and I am a strategic designer dedicated to contributing to a sustainable and innovative future through strategic problem-solving and design. I believe that whether a design is product- or service-oriented, it should empower people to move towards a better future. Though the future is dynamic and ever-changing, I am convinced that through teamwork, data-driven insights, and careful analysis of markets, companies, competitors, and consumers, a holistic vision can be developed to find the path to achieving the desired outcomes.

My design journey began at Delft University of Technology in the Netherlands, where I earned a Bachelor’s degree in Industrial Design and a Master’s in Strategic Product Design. The Master’s program emphasizes a contextual and user-centered approach to market integration, focusing on creating solutions that are not only innovative but also feasible, usable, and desirable. This training has equipped me to address complex challenges by blending creativity with analytical thinking and structured methodologies.

Studying at a technical university gave me a unique perspective, blending rigorous technical knowledge with creative design thinking. This combination shaped my approach, allowing me to balance functionality with real-world application, while focusing on data-driven problem-solving and iterative processes to arrive at effective solutions.

During my Master’s, I expanded my global perspective by studying abroad for a year. I spent a semester in Milan at Politecnico di Milano, where I deepened my design skills and even published a paper. I also studied Chinese at Fudan University in Shanghai, broadening my cultural understanding and adaptability to different environments—critical skills when working on projects that span diverse industries and global markets.

I also joined a Dream Team—a group of students who collaborated to design and build machines for competitions. My team developed two machines: a traditional electric race car and an autonomous electric race car. This experience refined my abilities to work cross-functionally and manage tight deadlines, while integrating various disciplines and stakeholders.

In my free time, I continually seek new challenges and opportunities to expand my skill set. I have become proficient in Adobe software, 3D modeling, and ventured into coding to create my own website. Beyond design, I enjoy activities that challenge my analytical thinking and problem-solving abilities, such as sports and games. These experiences have equipped me with the ability to quickly and effectively learn new skills and adapt to fast-paced environments.
